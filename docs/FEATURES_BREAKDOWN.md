# Core Features Breakdown – PakIndustry Insight

The initial development phase will focus exclusively on the core features listed below. Advanced AI features will be added later.

---

## 1. Centralized Resource Aggregation
Collect business and industry-related information from trusted sources (news websites, public APIs, YouTube channels, company websites, etc.) and store it in a structured format.

## 2. Industry Categorization
Automatically group companies and updates into relevant industries (e.g., Technology, Textile, Energy, Healthcare, etc.).

## 3. Search & Filter System
Allow users to search and filter companies or industries based on:
- Industry type
- Company name
- Location
- Keywords
- Trend tags

## 4. Company Profiles
Each company will have a profile containing:
- Name, sector, year founded, location
- Latest updates
- Growth indicators (basic)
- Related companies (manually or system-generated)

## 5. Trending Insights Dashboard
Show trending industries and top companies based on recent updates, mentions, or available data.

## 6. Basic AI Summaries (only for now)
AI will summarize content fetched from external sources to:
- Provide short industry/company summaries
- Extract key trend tags

## 7. Watchlist (Optional early feature)
Users can bookmark industries or companies to monitor.

## 8. Admin/Analyst panel (Phase 2+)
A dashboard to moderate resources, manage data input, and maintain accuracy.

---

⚠ Note:  
All advanced AI features (e.g., forecasting, risk assessment, skill-gap analysis, opportunity scoring, etc.) will be considered **after initial core platform implementation**.
